# Business Architecture

This layer contains business responsibility:

- business-engine PDRs
- business governance
- product and journey documentation that defines business ownership

## Navigation

- Start with the responsible `PDR-*` document for the Business Engine.
- Continue to the matching `ESR-*` in [`../30-engineering/`](../30-engineering/).
- Continue to RI material in [`../40-reference/`](../40-reference/).
- Continue to BVR evidence in [`../60-validation/`](../60-validation/).

## Rule

Business documents define business ownership. They do not absorb engineering, reference, standards, or validation responsibilities.
