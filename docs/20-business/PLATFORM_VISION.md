# AlpenTind Platform

## Purpose

AlpenTind Platform exists to support AlpenTind Guiding in its daily operational work.

It is not primarily a booking system, CRM or administration system.

**AlpenTind Platform is a Workspace Platform.**

Its purpose is to help people understand situations, coordinate work and make professional decisions.

## Vision

The platform shall become the natural workplace for AlpenTind.

Every Workspace should reduce uncertainty, increase understanding and support professional judgement.

## Design Philosophy

- Technology exists to support the work.
- The work never exists to support the technology.
- Reduce cognitive load.
- Support calm operational work.

## Workspace Platform Examples

- Arbetsdag
- Dialog
- Experience
- Person
- Guide

## Operational Goals

Help users:

- understand the current situation
- identify what requires attention
- understand available context
- perform the next meaningful action

Support professional judgement. Never replace it.

## Long-Term Vision

Create the calmest and clearest operational workspace possible.
